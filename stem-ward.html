Stem Ward

STEMWARD is a website that encourages students, particularly grade 10, to choose the STEM strand. In here we can give you information about the possible careers, advantages and experiences of STEM students that might help you in choosing the right strand. This website could also help you find ways to deal with the struggles of being a STEM student by sharing our experiences and by answering questions that you might have. It would be our pleasure to assist and guide you in your STEM path in the future. The name STEMWARD came from the words 'STEM' meaning Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics, and the word 'ward' that is used for giving a sense of direction. Hence, this website is made to develop STEM warriors that keeps on moving forward while facing the challenges of being a STEM student.
