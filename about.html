A learning and development strategy known as STEM incorporates the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Students learn crucial competencies such as problem-solving, creativity, critical analysis, teamwork, independent thinking, initiative, communication, and digital literacy through STEM (Government of Western Australia, n.d). Additionally, this strand encourages students to learn by engagement in projects that have real-world applications. This method enables pupils to participate actively while learning (Lutkevich, 2022). STEM helps students build strong work ethics, prepares them for careers, and creates professionals. It also includes a wide variety of experiences and abilities. A well-rounded education benefits greatly from each STEM component. Learners who study science gain a thorough awareness of their surroundings. It aids in their development of critical thinking and research skills. Young people are more equipped to operate in a high-tech workplace thanks to technology. Students can develop their problem-solving abilities and apply their knowledge to new tasks by studying engineering. People can use mathematics to examine information, fix mistakes, and consciously choose solutions (Study in the USA, 2021). Overall, STEM equips people with the abilities to prosper and adapt to this rapidly changing world. the right place.
